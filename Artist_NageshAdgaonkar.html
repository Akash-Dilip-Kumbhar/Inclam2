Nagesh Adgaonkar

Singer

Shree Nagesh Adgaonkar is a Hindustani Classical music vocalist. He started his musical training at the age of seven in Latur and began his training in Indian classical music under the guidance of Pt. Vitthalrao Jagtap, who trained him up to ‘Sangeet Visharad’.

● Education and Music Training :

He has successfully completed his musical training under Ustad Rashid Khan at his SBMT (Shakhri Begum Memorial trust) Academy. But he has continued to learn and gain his knowledge about Indian classical music at SBMT Academy.

Later, he moved to Lalit Kala Academy, enhancing his musical skills under the guidance of Dr. Rajendra Kandalgaonkar, at the Savitribai Phule Pune University. After finishing his education in classical music at Lalit Kala Academy he then moved to Kolkata to attain intensive training in a complete classical way under the guidance of great maestro ‘Ustad Rashid Khan’ of ‘Rampur Sahaswan Gharana’.
